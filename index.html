  • Why Son of a Gun

    Kuncho created “Son of a Gun” as a bold statement about survival, pressure, and rising above expectations. At a time when he felt underestimated and surrounded by challenges, the song became his way of reclaiming power—firing back lyrically at the doubt, hate, and obstacles around him. Featuring the late Daev Zambia, the track also reflects unity and mutual respect between two rising stars who believed in staying real and fearless, no matter the odds.

    Watch Now

  • Why Day by Day

    Kuncho Mindset made "Day by Day" as a personal reminder to stay grounded and focused, no matter how tough life gets. At the time, he was pushing through self-doubt, financial struggles, and the weight of big dreams—so the song became his way of speaking hope into existence. With every line, he affirms that growth is a process, and success doesn’t happen overnight—it happens day by day, with faith, hard work, and patience. It’s not just a song, it’s a mindset.
